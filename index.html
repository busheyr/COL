Welcome

Start here to learn what the Center offers and how to engage with us.

The Center for Online Learning supports the teaching mission of the Arkansas Colleges of Health Education by helping faculty design and deliver effective online and hybrid courses. We work with instructors to strengthen teaching practices, improve student engagement, and prepare graduates for the demands of modern healthcare professions.

Mission

To equip educators with the knowledge, tools, and support needed to create clear, well-structured, and engaging learning experiences, both online and in person, that prepare students for competent and compassionate practice in the health sciences.

Vision

To build a learning environment where technology and sound pedagogy reinforce one another, where faculty continue to grow as teachers, and where every course helps students develop the skills and judgment required in today‚Äôs healthcare workforce.
